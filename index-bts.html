About Me

A young, aspiring developer just itching to build cool and useful stuff! I like fantasy football, comedy, and beer. Oh, and tech of course! I've studied entrepreneurship for four years at Quinnipiac University and am continuing to learn how to program here with the fun folks at Rutgers.

I've previously worked at a business incubator where a lot of the businesses taking off were tech-related. It made me want to learn code, so I self-taught myself and continued to learn more. Building some of my first things, like this site, have been fun for me.

It's going to be quite the experience. I look forward to becoming pretty good at what I do, and then hopefully building cool things for people later on!
